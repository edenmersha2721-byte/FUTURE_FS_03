---

## 2. The problem

Most salons in Ethiopia still run bookings manually, and it costs them real money:

- **Missed & double bookings** — appointments taken by phone or in a notebook lead to
  overlaps, forgotten slots, and clients turned away.
- **No-shows** — no reminders means clients forget, leaving expensive gaps in the day.
- **Phone tag** — staff waste time answering "are you open?" and "how much is X?" instead
  of serving clients.
- **No online presence** — clients can't see services, prices, or availability, and can't
  book after hours (when most people actually plan their week).
- **Zero data** — the owner has no record of top services, busiest hours, or repeat
  clients to make decisions.
- **Reputation is invisible** — happy clients have nowhere to leave a review that would
  attract new clients.

**The result:** lost revenue, wasted staff time, and a brand that looks smaller than it is.

---

## 3. The solution

**Amra Beauty** is a custom web platform that gives a salon a professional online storefront
**and** a full back-office management system — in one product.

Two experiences, one platform:

**For clients**
- Browse services, prices, and durations on a polished, branded website.
- Book an appointment 24/7 from any phone or computer.
- Get email confirmations and reminders automatically.
- Reschedule a booking themselves (subject to the salon's approval).
- Submit a "look" / inspiration photo when they want something not on the menu.
- Leave a star rating and review after their visit.

**For the salon (admin dashboard)**
- See and manage every appointment in one place; confirm, complete, or cancel.
- Add and edit services, categories, prices, durations, and photos.
- Set business hours and closed dates so clients can only book real, open slots.
- Approve or reject client reviews before they appear publicly (reputation control).
- Run promotions and manage the photo gallery.
- Get notified the moment a new booking or review comes in.

| No-shows | Automatic email confirmations & reminders |

| Invisible online | A branded, premium website that sells the salon 24/7 |
| No reputation engine | Moderated reviews build trust and pull in new clients |
| No insight | Every booking, service, and client recorded in one dashboard |

**Bottom line:** more bookings captured, fewer gaps in the day, less admin time, and a
brand that looks established and trustworthy.

## 5. Target market

- **Primary:** established single-location salons and spas in Addis Ababa and other cities
  that already have steady clients and want to look and operate more professionally.
- **Secondary:** barbershops, nail studios, makeup artists, and small beauty chains.
- **Expansion:** any appointment-based local business (clinics, physiotherapy, tutoring)
  — the same platform adapts with minimal change.

---

## 6. What makes it different

- **Ownership, not rent.** The salon owns the code and its data — no monthly SaaS fee and
  no per-booking commission eating into margins.
- **A business system, not just a website.** Competitors offer a landing page; this is a
  full booking + management platform.
- **Built for the local market.** Priced and positioned for Ethiopian salons, in a market
  where most competitors only sell basic static sites.
- **Production-grade quality.** Real authentication, an admin/customer split, email
  notifications, review moderation, and scheduling logic — not a template.

## 7. Business model & pricing

Positioned as a **one-time custom build with code ownership**, plus optional paid add-ons.

**Core platform (one-time):**
- **Ask:** 60,000 ETB
- **Expected close:** 45,000–50,000 ETB
- **Floor:** 40,000 ETB

This reflects a real custom web application — booking workflow, admin dashboard, customer
dashboard, service management, review moderation, notifications, and business-hours logic —
not a basic website, which in the local market typically ranges around 18,000–25,000 ETB.
The gap is justified: this is a system that runs the business, not a page that describes it.

**Optional add-ons (priced separately):**
- Deployment & domain/email setup
- Online payment integration
- SMS reminders
- Ongoing support & maintenance (monthly retainer)
- Multi-branch / staff-account expansion

> **Positioning line:** "This is a custom booking and salon-management platform that you
> own — not a monthly subscription."

## 8. Technology & reliability (brief, for credibility)

- **Frontend:** React — fast, modern, mobile-friendly interface.
- **Backend:** Node.js/Express REST API with secure authentication.
- **Database:** PostgreSQL — reliable, structured storage of clients, bookings, and services.
- **Email:** transactional email for confirmations and reminders.
- The architecture is standard, maintainable, and ready to scale to more services and clients.

---

## 9. Roadmap (where it goes next)

- **Phase 1 (done):** Booking, dashboards, services, reviews, notifications, business hours.
- **Phase 2:** Online payments & deposits to cut no-shows further.
- **Phase 3:** SMS reminders and a loyalty/points program.
- **Phase 4:** Multi-branch support and per-staff calendars.
- **Phase 5:** Analytics dashboard — revenue, busiest hours, top services, repeat clients.

This pitch is backed by a working, full-stack product I built during the internship, not a
mock-up.

- **What it is:** a complete salon booking and management platform with a public client
  website, a customer dashboard, and an admin dashboard.
- **My role:** full-stack development — designing the database, building the backend API,
  and building the frontend interface, then connecting them into one working system.

- **Features delivered:** appointment booking with conflict-free scheduling, service &
  category management, business hours and closed dates, customer self-reschedule,
  inspiration/look requests, a photo gallery, promotions, in-app notifications, and a

- **What I learned:** turning a real business's day-to-day problems into software features,
  designing a data model around a real workflow, and thinking about the product as
  something a client would pay for and own — including how to price and position it.
- **Status & honesty:** the core platform is built and demo-ready. Production launch items —
  deployment, online payment integration, and domain-verified email — are scoped as clearly
  defined next steps (see the roadmap and paid add-ons), not hidden gaps.

### Appendix — Live demo script (suggested order)

1. Open the homepage → scroll services, gallery, testimonials.
2. Book an appointment as a client → show the confirmation email.
3. Log in as admin → show the new booking appear; confirm it.
4. Add a new service with a price and photo → show it live on the site.
5. Submit a review as a client → approve it as admin → show it publish to the homepage.
6. Set business hours / a closed date → show unavailable slots disappear from booking.
